What are Design Patterns?
-> There are solutions to solve commonly occuring problem in software design. using
 these patterns and customizing to solve our problem

There are Three types of Design Patterns:
1. Creational Design Patterns: 
    -> Provides Object Creation Mechanisms for flexibility and re-usability

2. Structural Design Patterns:
    -> Explains how to assemble objects and classes together into large structure
    while keeping the structure fexible and re-usable.

3. Behavioral Patterns:
    -> These Patterns provide ways for effective communication and assignments
    between the objects
